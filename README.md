# Fahkle

A pass and play dice game for up to four players, human or bot. Push your luck, bank what you can, and try not to Fahkle.

## How to play

- Roll all six dice.
- Set aside at least one scoring combination, then either bank your turn or roll the rest again for more.
- Bank all six dice in a turn and you get hot dice: all six come back into play and your turn score carries on.
- Roll with nothing scoring and you Fahkle, losing everything you hadn't banked that turn.
- First to the target score triggers a final round for everyone else, then highest score wins.

Full scoring breakdown is in the game itself, under the i button.
