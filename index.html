How to score

The basics

Roll all 6 dice. Set aside at least one scoring combo, then choose to roll the rest again or bank your turn. If a roll has no scoring dice at all, that's a Fahkle — you lose everything you hadn't banked yet, and play passes on.

Hot dice

Bank all 6 dice in a turn — in one roll or across several — and all 6 come back into play. Your turn score carries over, so you keep pushing your luck with a fresh set.

Getting on the board

If the house rule is switched on, your first banked turn of the game must be worth 500 points or more before it counts.

Winning

Once a player banks a turn that takes them past the target score, everyone else gets exactly one more turn. Highest total once that final round finishes wins.
